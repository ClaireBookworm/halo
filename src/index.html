The world is in need of face masks, as healthcare workers and other workers are in contact with people constantly in the day.

Today, the coronavirus is affecting more people than ever before. One big factor in keeping safe are face masks, which prevent the spread of disease.



While many people are staying at home and practicing social distancing™, many others are unable to stay home. They may be healthcare workers, right at the frontlines protecting us from the disease, or hourly workers working at grocery stores and whatnot to make sure we can all have the resources to stay safe today.
Research shows that face masks can reduce the chance of a patient from spreading the virus, as well as protect people from contracting the virus from the environment.
We are working with 3M factories to get more masks in production. As a 501(c)(3) non-profit your donations will go 100% torwards the goal, and all donations are tax-deductible.
